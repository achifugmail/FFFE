Fantasy Football Game Rules

Squad Composition

  • Your squad is selected via a draft process. Each user picks players in turn.
  • Each squad consists of a fixed number of players per position:
    • Goalkeepers (GK): usually 2
    • Defenders (DEF): e.g. 5
    • Wing Backs (WB): e.g. 2
    • Defensive Midfielders (DM): e.g. 2
    • Attacking Midfielders (AM): e.g. 4
    • Forwards (FW): e.g. 3
  • Exact numbers may be set by your league admin.
  • No two squads can own the same player at the same time.

Draft Process

  • The draft is turn-based. When it is your turn, you may select any available player.
  • Once a player is drafted, they are unavailable to other squads.
  • The draft continues until all squads are full.

Transfers

  • Transfers may be allowed between gameweeks, depending on league settings.
  • Players can only be added if they are not already in another squad.
  • Squad composition rules must always be respected.
